I'm Owen!

My name is Walker Widdis, but I go by my middle name, Owen! I am originally from Metro Detroit and am pursuing a degree in Experience Architecture with a minor in Computer Science at Michigan State University. Outside of the classroom and workspace, I love watching baseball (go Tigers!), playing volleyball, and solving Rubik's Cubes!

Fewest Moves Solving Seminar: Rubik's North American Championship 2022

In the Summer of 2022, I had the opportunity to give an informational seminar about Rubik's Cube Fewest Moves solving (FMC for short) at the biannual Rubik's North American Championship. With the help of my good friend AJ Kamal, we broke down the different strategies and techniques of FMC. I am very passionate about FMC and I was so grateful to have the opportunity to speak about it!
