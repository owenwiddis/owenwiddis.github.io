Rubik's Cube Fewest Moves Solving

One of my favorite aspects of the Rubik's Cube is the Fewest Moves Challenge (FMC for short). This is an event that is held by official World Cube Association, a global organization that officiates official Rubik's Cube competitions. I have been competing in WCA competitions since 2015 and having been practicing FMC since 2018. After many years of research, practice, and competing in FMC, I have achieved a top 10 world/top 5 in North America ranking for both a mean of 3 solves and a single solve, and Michigan State record for mean of 3 solves. I was also able to win CubingUSA Nationals 2023 in FMC, making me the current US National Champion!

Fewest Moves podium from US Nationals 2023 in Pittsburgh, PA
